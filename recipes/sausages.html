Bourbon Little Smokies

Description

This is a simple sausage recipe perfect for a quick dinner. It has 34 grams of protein and many calories, and my grandmother makes smokies this way

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups ketchup
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup bourbon whiskey
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 (14 ounce) package beef smoky cocktail sausages

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, whisk the ketchup, brown sugar, bourbon whiskey, and water together until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Stir in the cocktail sausages, and place over medium heat.
  3. Bring the sausages and sauce to a simmer; serve.
